Chilli Prawn Pasta

Description

A simple recipe for a tomato-based pasta sauce with chilli and prawns.

Ingredients

  • Packet of prawns
  • 1 tin Tomato Polpa
  • 1 Chopped chilli
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 500g Spaghetti
  • Handful of ripped fresh basil

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in a separate pan
  2. Fry off prawns in olive oil for some 30 seconds, moving in pan frequently
  3. Throw in garlic and chilli, allowing this to cook alongside the prawns
  4. Once prawns almost done, throw in can of tomato and allow to simmer and reduce
  5. once reduced, throw in basil leaves, wait until wilting, and toss with pasta
  6. Serve.
