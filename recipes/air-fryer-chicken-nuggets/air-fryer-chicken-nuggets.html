Air Fryer Chicken Nuggets

Description

This is a recipe born of my crazy mind. A wild combination of complex techniques and flavours - this is a dish for the upper echelon of beings walking upon this mortal soil. If you get to the end of this recipe without your mind being warped and your eyes bursting from raw beauty, you are permitted to attempt to replicate it.

Ingredients

  • Packet of nugs (preferably dino)

Steps

  1. Place nuggets into air fryer and cook for time specified on packet.
